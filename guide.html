Getting Started

First Steps

You begin with 100 cash. Use these commands immediately:

/daily - Claim 2,500 coins every 24h (16.8h premium)

/work - Earn 1,000 coins every 6h (4.2h premium)

/vote - Get coins + items every 12h per platform

Banking Strategy

Deposit money ASAP to protect from robberies:

25k Default Capacity

Use Bank Card for +100k capacity or Vault Key for +500k capacity.

Your bank is 100% safe from robberies!

Premium Advantage

Premium users get these exclusive benefits:

  • 30% reduced cooldowns on ALL commands
  • 20% more XP from every action
  • Double farming plots (10 vs 5)
  • Premium daily (3,750 coins + bonus item)
  • PeepMine benefits (15% more coins, higher caps)
  • Sell items for 100% value (vs normal 50%)

Safety First

Never carry large amounts of cash! Robberies can steal up to 40% of your cash — always deposit immediately after earning.

Bank = 100% safe from all robberies. Default capacity is only 25,000 — buy a Bank Card (+100k) or Vault Key (+500k) as soon as possible.

Robbery cooldown: Attackers must wait 18h (12.6h premium) before robbing again — use that window to farm and bank safely.

Watch out for geared robbers: A Safe Cracker gives 65% success rate, and combined with a Robbery Potion it reaches 90% — do not give them a reason to target you.

Protect yourself: Use a Protection Potion for 4 hours of full immunity — stack multiples before big earning sessions like harvesting Rare Flowers or claiming vote rewards.

Rule of thumb: Keep less than 10% of your total wealth as cash at any time.

PeepMine System

How It Works

Core Mechanics

PeepMine is a passive income system that generates peepcoins over time

Mine Level: Determines base mining rate (starts at level 1)

Miners: Workers who collect peepcoins (starts with 1)

Mining Rate: Peepcoins generated per second per miner

Formula: mining_rate Ã— miners = peepcoins per second

Collection & Storage

Use the "Collect Peepcoins" button to transfer stored coins to inventory

Regular storage: 1,000,000 peepcoins

Premium storage: 2,000,000 peepcoins

Tip: Collect regularly to avoid hitting storage cap

Mine Upgrades

Increase mine level to boost your mining rate

Cost: current_level Ã— 1,600 peepcoins

Benefit: +0.002 mining rate per upgrade

Max level: 100

Premium: 15% discount on upgrades

Hiring Miners

Add miners to multiply your income

Cost: current_miners Ã— 1,600 peepcoins

Regular limit: 100 miners

Premium limit: 150 miners

Premium: 15% discount on hiring

Prestige System

Reset progress for permanent boosts

Regular requirement: Mine level 100

Premium requirement: Mine level 90

Regular boost: +15% per level (max 5 levels = 75%)

Premium boost: +25% per level (max 7 levels = 175%)

Commands

/peepmine - Open your PeepMine interface

/peepminelb - View the top PeepMine leaderboard

Note: Leaderboard ranks by total peepcoins earned all-time
