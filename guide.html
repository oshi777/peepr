Game Strategies

Blackjack Basics

Always hit on 11 or less

Stand on 17+

Double down on 10/11 vs dealer 2-9

Split aces and 8s

Guess Game

Use binary search strategy:

1. Start with 50

2. Higher/lower → 25/75

3. Repeat until correct

Max profit: 5x with 5 guesses

Hangman

Most common letters:

E, A, R, I, O, T, N, S

Start with vowels first

Look for common endings (-ing, -tion)

Tic-Tac-Toe

First move: Take a corner

Second move: Take center if available

Strategy: Create two winning paths

Perfect play: Always ends in draw

Rock Paper Scissors

Psychology: Most people start with Rock

Counter: Lead with Paper

Pattern: Watch opponent's tendencies

Strategy: Mix up your choices randomly

Connect 4

Center control: Play middle columns first

Defense: Block opponent's three-in-a-row

Setup: Create multiple winning threats

Trap: Force opponent into bad positions
